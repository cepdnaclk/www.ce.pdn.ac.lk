<p class="whitespace-normal break-words">The Department of Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeniya is delighted to announce that one of its students, <strong>Mr. Adeepa Fernando</strong> from E18 batch has been recognized at the prestigious Migara Ratnatunga Trust Awards on Industrial Training for University Undergraduates, presented by the Institution of Engineers Sri Lanka (IESL).</p>
<p class="whitespace-normal break-words">Mr. Adeepa Fernando&nbsp;secured an award for his outstanding performance in the Computer Engineering category. The award was presented at the annual IESL Induction and Graduation Ceremony 2025, held at the Main Hall, BMICH on Monday, 28th July 2025.</p>
<p class="whitespace-normal break-words">The winners, selected from final-year undergraduates across all state university engineering faculties in Sri Lanka, were chosen based on their presentations to the IESL selection board, which evaluated them on the technical scope of their industrial training work, presentation skills, and their performance in the Q&amp;A session.</p>
<p class="whitespace-normal break-words">PeraCom is extremely proud of Mr. Fernando's achievement and wishes him continued success in his future endeavors. We hope that his accomplishment will inspire future generations to strive for excellence in their industrial training and professional careers.</p>

Vision and Mission Statements

The Department of Computer Engineering is committed to excellence in education and research, shaping future leaders in technology with global recognition.

Vision
To be a leading center for computer engineering education and research in Sri Lanka and the region, producing globally recognized graduates equipped to lead in modern computing and technology.
Mission
To deliver internationally aligned academic and research programs, fostering technical expertise, innovation, communication, and lifelong learning for successful careers in computer engineering.

Research and Impact

Our research drives innovation in cutting-edge technologies, addressing real-world challenges and fostering advancements in computer engineering. We collaborate globally to deliver impactful solutions that shape the future of technology.

Who we are...

The Association of Computer Engineering Students (ACES) was formed in August, 2001 as the students’ body of Computer Engineering undergraduates of the Faculty of Engineering with the aim of maximising students’ potential by organizing different activities and events that would bring out the best within themselves and make them prepared for the next technological revolution.
